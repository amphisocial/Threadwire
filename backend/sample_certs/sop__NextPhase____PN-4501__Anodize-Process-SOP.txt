STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURE — Type II Anodize (SOP-ANO-014)
Applies to: aluminum brackets incl. PN-4501. Company: NextPhase.

Scope: hard-coat anodize per MIL-A-8625 Type II, Class 2. Target coating thickness
0.0006–0.0010 in. Requires bath temperature 68–72°F, current density per spec, and
seal step. In-process verification: coating thickness on 20% sample per lot with
calibrated eddy-current gauge. Records retained per 21 CFR Part 11 (electronic
records, audit trail, retention 7 years).
